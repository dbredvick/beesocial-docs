# Managing your account

### Billing

BeeSocial partners with Stripe to handle billing. In order to update your subscription, you need to go to `/settings` and click "Configure".

### Account status

The status of your account will automatically be synced here as well.

The options are:

- TRIAL
- PRO
- ENT
- INACTIVE
