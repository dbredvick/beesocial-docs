# Uninstallation

<Callout type="warning" emoji="⚠️">
  Before uninstalling, be sure to cancel your subscription in the `/settings`
  page.
</Callout>

To uninstall, you have two options:

## Uninstall in Zoom

1. Login to your Zoom Account and navigate to the Zoom App Marketplace.
2. Click Manage > Installed Apps or search for the BeeSocial app.
3. Click the BeeSocial app.
4. Click Uninstall.

## Uninstall in BeeSocial

1. Navigate to /settings.
2. Click the "Delete" button next to "Delete your account."
3. Confirm the deletion by pressing "Deactivate" in the confirmation modal.
